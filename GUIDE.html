ABOUT THE GAME
'Flap Deez' is a game inspired by the classic game 'Flappy Bird'. The game that blew up the internet, but unfortunately was pulled out of the app store.

It is a game where the characters are instructed to jump by clicking anywhere on the game page to help them avoid oncoming blocks.


HOW TO PLAY
Firstly, select the level you'd like to challenge.

Tap anywhere to make your character fly. Avoid the blocks and glide through the map with the authors' faces! Every hole you glide through is worth a point!

Get 69 points to win! Goodluck!
